# PurrWallet Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2026

## Introduction

PurrWallet ("we," "our," or "the app") is a Cashu ecash wallet application. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information and data security.

## Information We Collect

### Locally Stored Data

PurrWallet is a local-first wallet application. The following data is stored only on your device:

- **Wallet Data**: Including your wallet balance, transaction history, ecash tokens, etc.
- **Wallet Keys and Seeds**: Used to access and manage your wallet
- **App Settings**: Including your personal preferences

**Important**: All wallet data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store this information on our servers.

### Device Permissions

To provide core functionality, the app may require the following permissions:

- **Camera Permission**: Used to scan QR codes for sending and receiving ecash
- **Network Access**: Used to connect to your chosen mint servers (such as local mints or Tor mints) for deposit and withdrawal operations

## Data Storage and Security

- All sensitive data (including wallet keys) is encrypted using the device's secure storage mechanisms
- Wallet data is stored entirely on your device locally and is never uploaded to any server
- We do not access, collect, or transmit your wallet data

## Data Sharing

We **do not** share, sell, or trade your personal information or wallet data with third parties. Your data is entirely under your control.

## Third-Party Services

When you use the following features, you may connect to third-party services:

- **Mint Servers**: When you perform deposit or withdrawal operations, the app connects to your chosen mint servers. These connections are under your control, and we do not collect or store this connection information.
- **Tor Network**: If you choose to use Tor mints, the app connects through the Tor network. We do not monitor or log these connections.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- You can delete the app at any time, which will delete all locally stored data
- You can use your wallet seed to recover your wallet
- You can choose whether to use network features

## Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any material changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or by updating this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us through:

## Consent

By using PurrWallet, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.
